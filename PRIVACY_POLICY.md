# TrustShield - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 28, 2026**

## Overview
TrustShield is a security application designed to protect users from phishing attacks and suspicious messages by analyzing incoming notifications.

## What We Access
TrustShield requests access to:
- **Notification Content**: We monitor notification text to detect phishing links and suspicious patterns
- **App Package Names**: We log which applications send notifications for security analysis

## What We Do NOT Do
- ❌ We do NOT store your personal messages
- ❌ We do NOT store contact information or sender details
- ❌ We do NOT share your data with third parties
- ❌ We do NOT sell your data to advertisers
- ❌ We do NOT transmit your messages to external servers

## Data Processing
- **Local Processing**: All analysis happens on your device
- **Temporary Processing**: Message content is analyzed in real-time and immediately discarded
- **Link Detection**: Only detected links are logged, not full message content
- **Anonymous Logging**: We log package names and detected threats, not personal identifiers

## Security Threats We Detect
- Suspicious URLs and phishing links
- Messages from unknown numbers
- Potentially malicious content patterns

## User Control
- You can disable the app at any time through Settings
- You can revoke notification access permission at any time
- You can view our logs via logcat (developer mode)

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting.

## Contact
For privacy concerns, please contact the developer through the Play Store listing.

## For Play Store Reviewers
This app uses `NotificationListenerService` for legitimate security purposes:
- Detecting phishing links in messages
- Identifying suspicious notifications from unknown sources
- Providing real-time threat detection
- No personal data is stored or transmitted
- All processing is local to the device
